Explore your Mappiness data

This website allows you to explore your data from the Mappiness iPhone app.

You will need your Mappiness data download link. To find it…

  1. Open the app on your phone, and go to “My happiness”.
  2. Tap the download icon at top right.
  3. If “data download” is turned off, turn it on.
  4. Your data download link will look something like this: https://mappiness.me/3kkq.pk7d.23wb

  5. That doesn’t seem to be a valid download link

Note: This site does not save your data anywhere. Your data is only loaded within your own browser and forgotten once you leave this page.
